Food & Beverage Use Cases

Product Quality Management

Beverage Manufacturers can predict the chemical quality of its products hours to days in advance. This way, they can intervene before products spoil during the fermenting process. Reduce the amount of product thrown away over the course of a year with our beverage solutions.

Food Manufacturers can predict sales volume to optimize shipping. They can predict equipment problems to increase the reliability of their plant. They can also predict the quality of raw materials they are about to purchase so they can make better purchasing decisions.

Utilities Use Cases

Electricity Demand Management

Electric Power Utilities can predict power demand, even in areas without advanced metering installed. This way they can optimize power purchasing and distribution while minimizing power shortages over the course of a year.

Manufacturing Use Cases

Minimize Waste in Your Supply Chain

Manufacturers can predict sales volume to optimize how to distribute products, what product mix to manufacture, and how much raw material inventory to keep on hand. This can reduce costs related to shipping and procurement. This can also increase revenue by capitalizing on unforeseen sales opportunities.

Public Sector Use Cases

Resource Planning and Emergency Response

Governments can quickly see patterns in terms of what resources are lacking in certain neighborhoods. This enables city and state leaders to optimize resource planning to improve the overall quality of life in their regions.

Government agencies that oversee transportation, power & water, and emergency response can predict adverse weather affects on their operations several hours or days in advance. This helps them initiate timely contingency plans before it is too late. Ensure that your front line staff have enough time to intervene with the help of our public sector solutions.
